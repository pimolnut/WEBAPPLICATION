Seasons in Bangkok

The weather in Bangkok is tropical. The country has three main seasons namely summer which extends from March to June, the monsoon or rainy season from July to October and the relatively cooler winter season which runs from November to February.

The winter is the best time to visit Bangkok. The days tend to be cooler and the evenings much more pleasant. If one is looking to spend time at the beaches or taking a boat cruise, then summer is possibly the best time to visit though bear in mind it can get beastly hot.

The origin of the word Bangkok

Back before we knew Bangkok In the past, this place was named "Bangkok." There is evidence from antiquities that Bangkok city was not recently an important city during the Ayutthaya period. But it has been important since the Ayutthaya period. In sea maps and ancient maps made by foreigners since the Ayutthaya period, The location of Thonburi appears in the name of "Bangkok" as well, spelled as Bangkok, Bancoc, Bancok, Banckok, Bankoc, Banckock, Bangok, Bancocq, Bancock and on some sheets write the word "Siam" which means Siam. At the location of Bangkok

While Judia, Odia, Juthia, Ajothia, and Odiaa are preceded by the location of Ayutthaya. Therefore, Bangkok is a symbol of the Kingdom of Thailand itself. As for the word Bangkok, which is spelled as the name of the city of Bangkok or the current Rattanakosin, It is a word that the supreme pontiff in Christianity in Ayutthaya Used when writing to Bangkok Every time he wrote a report to the headquarters in Paris and has been used continuously until the Rattanakosin period and today
